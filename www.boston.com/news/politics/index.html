Fresh off a win in high-profile Ohio senate race, Sarah Benzing hired to manage Edward Markey’s Senate campaign

US Representative Edward J. Markey, who is running in a special election to succeed Senator John F. Kerry, has hired a campaign manager fresh off a win in a high-profile senate race in Ohio, according to a statement from the campaign Saturday. Sarah Benzing managed the campaign of incumbent Democratic Senator Sherrod Brown, who defeated Republican Josh Mandel in a battleground race that attracted huge waves of outside spending. According to The Hill, a Washington-based newspaper covering politics, Benzing grew up in the small western Iowa town of Neola.

President Obama meets with Hamid Karzai, cites “historic moment” for troop drawdown

Edward Markey hires Elizabeth Warren’s two top fund-raisers to work on his Senate campaign

Representative Edward J. Markey has hired the two staffers who helped Senator Elizabeth Warren raise a record-setting $42 million last fall to fill the same role for his planned US Senate campaign. Michael Pratt, who served as Warren’s national finance director, and Colleen Coffey, who headed fund-raising in Massachusetts, were labeled a “dynamic duo” by one Democrat. Pratt and Coffey helped Warren outraise not only her Republican rival, then-Senator Scott Brown, but every other congressional candidate in the country.

State Senator Ben Downing decides against US Senate campaign to replace John Kerry

State Senator Benjamin Downing has decided against waging what he concluded was a longshot campaign for US Senate. The Pittsfield Democrat issued a statement at midday saying he had been heartened by the response word of his possible candidacy had received, but sobered by the daunting financial challenge he faced. US Representative Edward J. Markey, a Malden Democrat, has already said he will run if, as expected, Senator John F. Kerry resigns to become secretary of state

Gerard Leone’s departure as Middlesex DA hints at low-key personality, political persona

One of the most coveted political jobs in Massachusetts is district attorney of Middlesex County, the most populous in the state and covering a broad swath of Massachusetts that includes such diverse communities as Cambridge and Lowell. Scott Harshbarger, Tom Reilly, and Martha Coakley all transitioned from Middlesex DA to state attorney general. All that explains why it was a shock to the political system Gerard Leone announced to his staff that he would not seek reelection in 2014
