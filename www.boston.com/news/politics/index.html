Governor Deval Patrick heading to the U.S. Virgin Islands for vacation

Governor Deval Patrick will be headed out of town for a week of vacation in the U.S. Virgin Islands, starting Saturday, according to his spokesman. Patrick last traveled for vacation in December, a surprise long-weekend trip to Paris for his wife’s birthday.

Senator John Kerry to host Barack Obama fundraiser in Georgetown Monday

Senator John Kerry and his wife Teresa Heinz Kerry will host a fundraiser for President Obama’s re-election campaign Monday evening at their Georgetown home, according to an Obama campaign official. Vice President Joe Biden will headline the event, which is expected to draw about 75 guests. Kerry, chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee which Biden once headed as well, has had a long history of raising money for Democratic campaigns.

Scott Brown’s new radio ad praises Elizabeth Warren, takes credit for People’s Pledge

Republican US Senator Scott Brown is taking his bipartisanship rhetoric to the next level today – with a radio ad praising his Democratic opponent Elizabeth Warren on their agreement to curb the influence of outside spending in the heated campaign. But even as the ad praises Warren for agreeing with him, it takes full credit for initiating the pact, which may rekindle debate over whose idea it was to forge the so-called People’s Pledge to begin with.

Former legislative aide enmeshed in House chamber incident seeks office herself

A former State House aide who became swept up in a purported scandal last year has decided to run for the House herself. Diana DiZoglio, a Methuen Democrat, says she is not running because of the incident but informed by it. She also says she hopes to serve as an example to other young women who have had similar life-altering experiences. “It’s taught me that if you really want to do something, don’t let anything deter you,” said DiZoglio.

Elizabeth Warren to star in Obama campaign documentary

Elizabeth Warren’s Hollywood ties have been mocked by Republicans. Now, she’s about to get her own star turn in a Democratic movie. The Democrat who is seeking to unseat US Senator Scott Brown has scored an apparently prominent role in a slick documentary produced for President Obama’s reelection team. The film is directed by Academy Award winner Davis Guggenheim, who also directed “An Inconvenient Truth.” It’s narrated by Tom Hanks.
